About Me

I've always been driven by a need to understand how things work under the hood. I'm currently in my senior year at Louisiana State University, wrapping up my B.S. in Computer Science with a focus on cybersecurity and a minor in business administration. For me, the sweet spot in tech is understanding not just how to write the code, but how to secure the entire system and see how it actually impacts a business.

Right now, I get to put that into practice every day as a Technical Support Agent for LSU. It’s hands-on experience where I’m untangling real-world hardware, software, and enterprise network issues for students and faculty.

When I’m off the clock and away from the IDE, my interests stay pretty hands-on. You can usually find me upgrading and optimizing my custom PC build, dialing in a new project on my 3D printer, or tracking the financial markets.
